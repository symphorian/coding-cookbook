Deleting a Pantheon Site

Sometimes, you just want to start again from a clean slate

  1. First off, make sure you're VERY sure you want to delete your site. This process can't be reversed once you kick it off.

    You're really sure you want to delete your site? Okay, then let's continue.

    If you haven't already, log in to your Pantheon account, and navigate to the Sites tab in the left navbar.

    From here, click on the name of the Pantheon site that you want to delete.

  2. This will take you to the Pantheon Site Dashboard for that site.

    To begin the process of deleting your site, click the Settings cogwheel icon in the top-right of the screen.

  3. This will open up the Settings popup.

    From here, click on the tab labeled "Delete site".

  4. When you navgiate to the "Delete site" tab, click the big red [Delete Site] button.

  5. You might think that clicking [Delete Site] would delete your site...but no.

    They have an additional safeguard in place to prevent accidental deletion of your site: you need to manually type out the name of the site you want to delete.

    They want to be REALLY sure that you actually want to delete your site.

  6. Once you've typed out the name of your Pantheon site EXACTLY, the [Delete This Site] button will become enabled.

    Click that button to truly kick off the process of deleting your Pantheon site.

  7. After clicking the [Delete This Site] button, the popup will close, and you'll be returned to the Pantheon Site Dashboard.

    For a few moments, it won't look like anything is happening, but behind the scenes, a script is running on the web server to start deleting your site.

    After a few seconds pass, you'll see a little spinning/loading icon next to the Workflows link towards the top of the screen. That indicates that the deletion process is running.

    Just wait a little longer, and the deletion process will soon be complete.

  8. Eventually, you'll be brought back to the Sites tab of your Pantheon account.

    You should see that the site you wanted to delete is no longer in the Sites list.

Congrats! You've successfully deleted your Pantheon site!
