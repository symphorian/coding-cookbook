Additional Examples

The text-decoration property allows you to add line "decorations" to text.

The available line styles are: none, underline, overline, and line-through.

Example of text-decoration: none;

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Debitis fugiat, ad minus pariatur ducimus exercitationem facilis sit fugit commodi aperiam maxime rem nisi voluptatibus et suscipit repellat dolorem vel iure?

Example of text-decoration: underline;

Lorem ipsum, dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Atque maxime vero quibusdam facilis provident, quaerat fugiat dolore aut minus nulla dolorem molestiae. Consequuntur asperiores reprehenderit eos voluptatum cupiditate eaque quod.

Example of text-decoration: overline;

Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Id, rem dignissimos repellat officiis facilis molestias eius voluptas odit. Similique placeat nam velit quae nostrum consequatur, distinctio consequuntur fugit hic reiciendis.

Example of text-decoration: line-through;

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Iusto sit quis laboriosam officia laudantium distinctio. Non veritatis maxime officiis ea, nobis dolor magnam, libero nostrum animi sed repellendus nihil at.
