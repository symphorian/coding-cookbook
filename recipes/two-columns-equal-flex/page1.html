Asymmetrical Example

Here's an example of what an asymmetrical design might look like where you have a large title or some sort of attention-getter on one side, and then additional detail related to the large content on the other side.

It's almost similar to a slideshow, where you have a single slide with only the title of the next section on it, followed by several slides of content.

Asymmetrical designs can be especially effective if you repeat them but flip the asymmetry. It helps provide a nice balance to the page while keeping the layout from getting stale.
