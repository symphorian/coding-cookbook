Gallery - Vertical Masonry (columns)

To build our vertical masonry gallery this time, we're going to use a CSS property that is practically ancient relative to the speed that technology advances; the property is simply called "columns".

Although this property has been around for a long time, it will allow us to create a flexible vertical masonry gallery, avoiding most of the shortcomings of other gallery approaches, such as cropping images or not being able to adapt to different screen sizes.
